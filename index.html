What do you mean Arts Fire? Are you sure it isn't Farts Ire? No, it's Arts Fire. Some poeple in this country are in a very meek, placating mood. They live afraid of making offense, of outraging someone. So their lives are dominated by a cloud of cacophonic voices telling them to be careful what they say, who they associate with, what they eat, where they live, and a legion of other niggling worries. It's a hell of a way to live.

IMHO, the best art is triggering. It evokes feels. So it must be fearless. My vision for Arts Fire is a company that supports a community of creatives who produce good work even if it goes against the grain, maybe especially because it goes against the grain.

And I'll stop here because what's next is a bunch of preaching on my opinion of going against the grain. Arts Fire is just me at the moment. A lot improves once there is one more and one more and one more creative, many more creatives building a community of cranky creatives.
